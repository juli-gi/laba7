How to help animals?

We believe that all animals have rights and deserve to have their best interests accounted for, whether it benefits people or not.

The mistake of many is that they are accustomed to dividing the killing of animals into good and bad.

Attitude towards animals

However, many are calm about the slaughter of agricultural fur animals, killing for the sake of food or clothes.

For some reason, some people are convinced that if an animal is raised for meat or fur, then it is not has the right to life.
